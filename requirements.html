The below are the supported system requirements, lower specs may work but we can't promise functionality. This is partially due to lack of equipment to test with.
Note: These are the requirements for mcedit-unified, for the requirements for mcedit 2 you may continue use this as a reference until the requirements are available.

Windows Mac Linux
Operating System XP SP3 or newer OSX 10.6.8 Snow Leopard^ or newer Any Linux variant with python 2.7
Processor Intel Core 2 Duo or better^^ Intel Core 2 Duo or better^^ 2.5ghz Pentium 4 or better
Ram 2GB or higher (4GB is better)
Video Card Any fully OpenGL 1.1 compatible graphics card
Free Disk Space 1 - 1.5GB

Notes:
^Not thoroughly tested with versions below 10.9 Mavericks.
^^This is due to the speed on how fast mcedit-unified can run and any other programs that are currently on without crashing.
If you currently are running mcedit-unified on Intel Core 2 Duo or lower you may continue to do so, but you may recieve a crash on newer released versions of mcedit-unifed.
